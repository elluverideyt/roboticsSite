About Us

Our first team was founded in 2008, and we entered our rookie year with only four members.

Valley Christian Schools' (VCS) Robotics program is part of the world-wide organization known as FIRST® Robotics, “The Ultimate Sport for the Mind™.” FIRST® (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a nonprofit organization whose goal is to inspire and educate young people in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM).

Since our founding, VCS Robotics has grown to include two FTC teams - #2844, "Valley X Robotics," our varsity team, and #12841, "+3 Robotics," our JV team. Our FRC team, #8848, "Blu CRU" was added in 2022.

Our Teams

Valley Christian has two FTC Teams, and one FRC Team.

Each year, our teams design, CAD, build, program, and test our robots starting with only a common set of rules to play in a themed, head-to-head challenge.
Our teams also work to develop community partnerships for support, and work to promote STEM in our local community. Valley Christian is proud to feature three robotics teams!

Outreach

Outreach is a very important part of FIRST® events as it brings more people into the sport, and open up oppurtunities to join.

This year we connected with our community and spread the word about STEM and FIRST® Robotics in numerous ways. When reaching out, we discuss the Engineering Design process and demonstrate our FTC and FRC robots.
