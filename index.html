About VCS Robotics

Valley Christian Schools' (VCS) Robotics program is part of the world-wide organization known as FIRST® Robotics, “The Ultimate Sport for the Mind™.” Each year, our teams design, CAD, build, program, and test our robots starting with only a common set of rules to play in a themed, head-to-head challenge.

Outreach & Connections

This year, we connected with our community and spread the word about STEM and FIRST® Robotics through presentations at schools, homecoming events, community fairs, and the Chandler Innovation Fair.

  • CTA Independence K–6 STEM classes
  • VCHS Homecoming & Senior Night
  • Arizona Community Church Country Fair
  • 13th Chandler Innovation Fair (1st place tie, $500 prize)

Service

Each year we host the FTC AZ Turquoise Qualifier at our Tempe campus, welcoming 32 teams to compete for awards and experience FIRST® values.
